Our Services

Hagen Landscapes is your full service landscape and maintenance provider. Below are some of our customer favorites!

Maintenance & Mowing

Tailored to both you and your property. We'll keep your lawn beautiful all season long with the highest quality weekly or bi-weekly mowing services. Our lawn division offers property contracts that include mulching, hedge pruning, tree pruning, weeding, leaf removal, and aeration.


Patios & Walkways

Whether you are looking to transform your backyard into an outdoor getaway or make the front of your home look more welcoming, Hagen Landscapes can translate your ideas into a reality. Re-imagining your backyard into a usable living space will add value and functionality to your home. Front and side entrance walkways will add curb appeal and bring new life to your home.

Plantings & Designs

The goal of Hagen Landscapes is to design a landscape that will grow with the needs of you and your family. We will set you up with a design that is not only aesthetically pleasing but also provides functionality throughout the seasons and years to come.

Tree Work

There is no job too big or too small for Hagen Landscapes. Extensive knowledge in tree and plant health care allows Hagen Landscapes to deliver quality, safe, clean, and efficient tree removal, which provides customers with affordable and skilled work.

Snow Removal

Because Hagen Landscapes is your full service landscape provider; we offer reliable and affordable snow removal to residential and commercial customers. When the weather is at its worst, we are at our best!

Drainage & Erosion

Poor drainage and erosion can be caused by improper property grading and or excess water runoff from storms, parking lots, driveways, and roofs. Whether it be regrading, redirecting downspouts, or installing a French drain system, we'll send out a specialist to diagnose and fix your issue.
