About Hagen Landscapes

Gage Hagen - Owner / Founder

For over a decade Gage Hagen and his team of landscape professionals have been providing families and businesses across the Northern Virginia and Metropolitan area with high quality, affordable, and functional landscapes to enjoy for years to come. Gage holds an Associate in Agriculture and a Bachelor of Science in Horticulture from Virginia Tech. His degrees along with extensive field experience have provided him with a strong understanding of plant material and landscape practices.
